The Middleman Crisis: Why India’s Artisans Are Disappearing

Market Isolation & Low Income

Lack of digital presence and logistics access forces artisans to rely on exploitative middlemen.

Impact: The average income for a rural artisan can be as low as **₹74 per day**. Middlemen often take **40-60%** of the final retail price, pushing artisans into poverty and debt.

Loss of Authenticity

Consumers have no way to verify a product's origin, and traditional techniques are lost to mass-market demands.

Impact: **90%** of Indian artisans operate informally, lacking the brand identity to compete against cheap, machine-made alternatives that flood the market.

Generational Decline

Low, inconsistent income discourages younger generations from continuing their family's craft heritage.

Impact: The United Nations estimates that **30%** of artisans in India have left the trade in recent years due to income instability, leading to an irreversible loss of cultural heritage.

A Massive, Digitizing Market

The Indian handicrafts market is on a steep growth trajectory, driven by e-commerce adoption.

Our Solution: The Hasti Kala Platform

This section details our product: a mobile-first, direct-to-consumer marketplace designed with and for artisans. Interact with the tabs to see how our core features directly address the problems of market access, fair pay, authenticity, and logistics.

Empowering Artisans with Simple Tech

Our platform removes digital barriers. Artisans can easily upload photos of their work, and our AI automatically suggests descriptions, materials, and tags. A voice-enabled interface supporting regional languages makes listing products and managing orders as simple as talking.

Fair and Instant Payments

We cut out the middleman entirely. Payments are instantly released to the artisan's bank account or UPI after product verification and shipping. A built-in digital ledger automates record-keeping and invoicing, replacing cumbersome manual bookkeeping.

Connecting Buyers to the Source

Every product page features an 'Artisan Profile' card with the artisan's photo, village, and the story behind the craft. This fosters an emotional connection and builds brand value. We also integrate verification for Geographical Indication (GI) tags to guarantee provenance and authenticity.

"Before Hasti Kala, I barely made enough to send my children to school. Now, I feel respected, and my craft is valued by the world. It means dignity, not just income." - Anjali, Weaver

Solving the Last-Mile Problem

Artisans shouldn't be logistics experts. We partner with local courier services for reliable pickup directly from the artisan's cluster. Our field agents provide pre-printed shipping labels and packaging support, making shipping effortless for the creator.
